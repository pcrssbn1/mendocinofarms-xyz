Frequently Asked Questions About Mendocino Farms

Customers regularly reach out with questions about our menu offerings, location details, dietary accommodations, and ordering processes. This page compiles the most common inquiries with detailed answers to help you make informed decisions about dining with us.

From understanding our vegan banh mi ingredients to finding specific location addresses like 600 N Shepherd Dr Houston TX 77007, we've organized information to address both general curiosity and specific needs. For broader information about our company history and values, visit our about page. To explore our full range of offerings, return to the home page.

What type of food does Mendocino Farms serve?

Mendocino Farms is a fast-casual restaurant chain specializing in gourmet sandwiches, salads, and soups made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. The menu features creative combinations that blend California cuisine with global flavors, such as Vietnamese-inspired banh mi sandwiches, Mediterranean-style grain bowls, and seasonal soups that change quarterly. Every item emphasizes ingredient quality, with many vegetables sourced from regional farms and proteins prepared fresh daily in each location's kitchen. The culinary team develops recipes that balance nutritional value with bold flavors, creating options that appeal to both health-conscious diners and those seeking indulgent comfort food.

Where are Mendocino Farms locations?

Mendocino Farms has multiple locations primarily throughout California, with some locations in other states like Texas and Washington. The California presence includes concentrations in Los Angeles (including the Mendocino Farms Fig at 7th and Hollywood locations), San Diego, Orange County, the Bay Area, and Sacramento regions including Roseville. Texas locations operate in Houston (such as the Heights location at 600 N Shepherd Dr Houston TX 77007) and Austin. Washington state has locations in the Seattle metro area, and Nevada has a presence in Las Vegas. The company operates approximately 35 locations as of 2024, all company-owned rather than franchised, which ensures consistent quality and experience across every restaurant.

Does Mendocino Farms have vegetarian options?

Yes, Mendocino Farms offers numerous vegetarian and vegan options including plant-based sandwiches, fresh salads, and seasonal vegetable dishes. The vegan banh mi at Mendocino Farms is a customer favorite, featuring marinated tofu with pickled daikon, carrots, cucumbers, cilantro, and jalapeños on a fresh baguette. Other popular vegetarian choices include the avocado and quinoa superfood salad, grilled vegetable sandwiches with house-made spreads, and seasonal offerings that highlight produce at peak freshness. The menu clearly marks vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options, and staff members receive training on ingredient compositions to help customers with dietary restrictions. Many non-vegetarian items can be modified by substituting proteins or removing specific ingredients to accommodate plant-based preferences.

Can I order Mendocino Farms online for delivery?

Yes, you can order Mendocino Farms online through their website or mobile app for pickup and delivery through various delivery platforms. The official website and mobile app offer the most direct ordering experience, allowing you to customize items, save favorite orders, and earn loyalty points with each purchase. Delivery is available through partnerships with major third-party platforms including DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub, though availability varies by location. Pickup orders can be scheduled in 15-minute increments, and the app notifies you when your order is ready. For catering orders serving 10 or more people, the website offers specialized ordering tools with 24-48 hour advance notice recommended for larger groups. Gift cards purchased online can be used for both in-person and digital orders.

What are Mendocino Farms hours?

Most Mendocino Farms locations are typically open daily from 11 AM to 9 PM, though hours may vary by location and should be verified for specific restaurants. High-traffic urban locations like Mendocino Farms 7th and Fig or the Marina Del Rey restaurant may extend hours during peak seasons or for special events. Some locations in shopping centers adjust their schedules to match mall hours, potentially opening at 10 AM or closing at 10 PM. Holiday hours differ from regular schedules, with most locations closing early on major holidays like Thanksgiving and Christmas Eve, and remaining closed on Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day. The website and mobile app provide location-specific hours, and calling ahead is recommended if you're planning to visit near opening or closing times, particularly on holidays.

Is Mendocino Farms a franchise?

No, Mendocino Farms operates as a company-owned chain rather than offering franchise opportunities. This business model allows the founding team and management to maintain direct control over quality standards, ingredient sourcing, employee training, and customer experience across all locations. While franchising enables rapid expansion, it often creates inconsistencies in execution and can dilute brand values—risks the company chose to avoid. The 2018 investment round that valued the company at approximately $85 million provided capital for expansion while preserving the company-owned structure. This approach mirrors strategies used by other quality-focused fast-casual brands like Sweetgreen and Shake Shack, which prioritized controlled growth over rapid franchising. For potential investors or business partners interested in the company, corporate development inquiries go through the main office rather than franchise departments.

What ingredients are in the Mendocino Farms chipotle vinaigrette?

The Mendocino Farms chipotle vinaigrette ingredients include chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, fresh lime juice, extra virgin olive oil, garlic, honey, Dijon mustard, and a proprietary blend of spices including cumin and black pepper. This dressing appears on multiple menu items including the Mexican-inspired salads and as an optional addition to sandwiches. The chipotle peppers provide smoky heat while the lime juice adds brightness and acidity, creating a balanced flavor profile that complements both greens and proteins. The vinaigrette is made fresh in small batches at each location and contains no artificial preservatives, which gives it superior flavor but requires refrigeration and limits shelf life. Customers with specific allergen concerns should note that the dressing contains honey (not vegan) and is prepared in kitchens that handle common allergens according to FDA food allergen guidelines. Bottles of the vinaigrette are available for purchase at most locations for customers who want to recreate the Mendocino Farms experience at home.
